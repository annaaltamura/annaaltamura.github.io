Garden
In Bloom

Conceptualized and crafted a native mobile app where you can buy plants. Designed to follow each platform's standard, created with a visual identity that reminds the values of the product. Providing the best shop experience solution and personalized plants care.

The aim of Amount is to help people to save money quickly in preparation for a big purchase or expenditure, keeping your budget balanced to reach your goals.

Research

To begin I had to determine the problem I was trying to solve for users. The first step was to research through competitor analysis, then, I aimed to define users stories. The next step was to ideate solutions through wireframe and once the prototype was compleated, I moved to validate usability to build the final screens

Wireframes

After the competitor research was completed. I moved to the wireframes to map out the functionalities. I wanted to create a style that has no big contrasts, delicate, with big white space between the elements, in order to match with the essence of the products.

Ideation

Indoor plants are a part of decoration and what gives them life is light. The colour palette is inspired by different lightning during the day that gives life to plant and is part of the room. The Get Inspired section helps users in searching the needed products, giving a preview of the product in a room.

Design

All products are listed with big pictures and a minimal effective design to give a more enjoyable shopping experience. After searching a product, users see the result in a single screen that allows them to read and decide to add the product to the cart. Also, you can turn notifications on and stay updated on your plants needs and take care of them with the guide kit.

Prototype

The App has also an Android version and they differ from each other because in detail they follow the different standards of each platform.

Conclusion

Amount offers accessible information to users that they receive via the tool, and clearly tells them what they can do to better save by an educational approach and intuitive functionalities
