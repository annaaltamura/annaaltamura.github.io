Garden In Bloom

Conceptualized and crafted a native mobile app where you can buy plants. Designed to follow each platform's standard, mixed with a visual identity that reminds the values of the product. Providing the best shop experience solution and personalized plants care.

The aim of Amount is to help people to save money quickly in preparation for a big purchase or expenditure, keeping your budget balanced to reach your goals.

Research

After studying multiple competitors such as Peak, Digs, Eco, Revolut, Digit and more. I defined the main features and layout of the App. Based on the competitor research, I created a user flow that reflects the user's progress from the launch of the app to the insight of the goals

Ideation

Typography and photography play a key roles in brand identity. The finances will be explained with big typography and minimal charts to make it understandable for every user. Amount has two main values that the brand identity has to show: the fintech, logical and precise side, along with the young and fiercely side. The font Fractul, thanks to its design, represents both of those values

Design

The home provides the latest and updated state of your total achievement and for more details, users can immediately click to go to the analytics section. The intuitive, clear and simple layout has been designed to help every user to understand and control their goals

Conclusion
